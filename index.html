About PraYuJa

Transforming tradition into innovation

PraYuJa revives Nepal's traditional remedy for gastritis, using over 30 herbs passed down from grandmothers, modernized for the world. Our product guarantees relief within 10 minutes of consumption and helps recover easily without the need for allopathic medicines.

With a gastritis prevalence of 79.3% in Nepal (2020-2023), our solution addresses a critical health issue while preserving indigenous knowledge and promoting sustainability.

Our mission is to create the perfect blend of innovation, entrepreneurship, and friendship while keeping environment and sustainability as our goal.
